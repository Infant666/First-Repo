The First Movie

According to Wikipedia, the oldest movie ever to be filmed was Roundhay Garden Scene. This is a short silent motion picture filmed by French inventor Louis Le Prince at Oakwood Grange in Roundhay Leeds on 14th October 1888

The people in the video appear to be liesurly walking around the garden or dancing. This is just a 2 second video which must have taken alot of effort to record it back then and was called a movie, unlike today where people post millions of reels which is also a short video just like the one made in 1888.
